360 Photograph From Fort Howard

Fort Howard is located along the Chesapeake Bay in Fort Howard, Maryland

According to a Baltimore County government run website, Fort Howard's historical significance is its connection with the largest invasion in United States History.

The British had landed 7,000 troops near the site that later became Fort Howard, as part of an effort to capture and burn Baltimore.

However, their effort was haulted when their popular commanding general was killed by American sharpshooters Daniel Wells and Henry McComas.
