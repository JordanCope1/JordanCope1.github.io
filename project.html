Maryland Tourism Industry Takes Hit Amid COVID-19 Pandemic

Amid the COVID-19 pandemic, the tourism industry across the country has taken a hit.

The State of Maryland is no exception.

In Maryland, the number of visitors has been on the decline well before the pandemic even started.

Needless to say, 2020-2021 did not provide hope for improvement.
